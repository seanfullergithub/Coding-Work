Instructions

Welcome to Type Rocket! A game that helps you improve/develop your typing skills. Your task is to guide the rocket to the top right of the screen by accurately typing out writing prompts as they appear. Each prompt must be typed exactly how it appears on the screen, spaces included. Typing prompts correctly will move the rocket forward while any errors will send it backward. If you get the rocket to the top right, you win! If it goes all the way back to the bottom left, you lose. Upon reaching the top right, you can view your completion time. Play again to see if you can beat your time. How fast and accurate can you type? Good Luck!
